Hybrid RAG combines dense vector retrieval with sparse keyword search (BM25)
and optional re-ranking. In this lab, documents are chunked, embedded with
Sentence-Transformers or OpenAI, stored in Qdrant/Chroma/pgvector, and retrieved
before LLM generation. The local fallback endpoint is Ollama at port 11434.

Key retrieval parameters:
- chunk_size: 500 tokens
- overlap: 80 tokens
- top_k: 3

For production, add access controls, audit logging, and PDPA-aligned data handling.
